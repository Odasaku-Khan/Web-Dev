What Does It Mean to Be Lazy?

Laziness, often described as a reluctance to put in effort, is a common human trait.
It manifests as procrastination or avoidance of tasks, even when completing them is necessary.
While it's often viewed negatively, laziness can sometimes stem from deeper issues such as burnout or lack of motivation.

Causes of Laziness

There are many reasons why people feel lazy.
Lack of clear goals or overwhelming tasks can make people feel stuck, leading to inactivity.
Physical factors like fatigue, poor nutrition, or lack of exercise can also play a role.
Similarly, emotional states like stress or boredom can sap energy and discourage action.
